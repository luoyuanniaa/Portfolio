便携剪刀Stick Scissors

Scissors that vanish into a pen-sized stick.像笔一样收纳的便携剪刀。

2024–26 · Portable tool便携工具 · Concept→Mechanism→CMF · nusign

Context背景

Slim, safe scissors actually worth carrying every day.日常可随身、又足够安全的纤薄剪刀。

Concept概念

Blades sheath inside a pen-slim cylinder; twist to deploy and lock.刀刃藏于笔形圆柱,旋转展开并锁定。

Split into body, carrier and lock; A/B tested action and grip.拆为机身、刀刃载体与锁定,A/B 验证开合与握持。

Outcome成果

A pocket stick with tactile detent feedback; calm blue with a colour range.带段位手感的口袋剪刀,沉静蓝并延展配色。

09 · Graduation毕业设计

Prompt — Thinking from the “Clip”Prompt —— 从「夹子」开始思考

毕业设计 · GenAI × 3D printingGraduation Project · 生成AI × 3D 打印

Rethinking the humble clip through generative AI and 3D printing.用生成式 AI 与 3D 打印,重新思考「夹子」这一日常之物。

2023 · Graduation project毕业设计 · GenAI × 3D printing · Academic学术

Context背景

AI and 3D printing are reshaping how products come to be. The project asks what design becomes when generative tools enter the everyday.AI 与 3D 打印正在改变产品被创造的方式;课题追问:当生成式工具进入日常,设计会变成什么。

Concept概念

Take the humble “clip” as a lens — simple and universal — and move from unconscious gesture to conscious form.以最朴素的「夹子」为切口——简单而通用——从无意识的手势,推进到有意识的形。

Development推进

Generate a family of clip forms with generative AI, then prototype them rapidly as 3D-printed colour samples.用生成式 AI 衍生出一整组夹子造型,再以 3D 打印彩色样件快速验证。

Outcome成果

A collection of clip-objects — and a method, “Prompt” — shown at the degree exhibition.一组夹子物件,加上一套方法「Prompt」,在毕业展完整呈现。

Draft written from your exhibition panels — send corrections or your full thesis text and I'll update.以上依据你毕业展展板所拟草稿——把修改意见或毕设正文发我即可更新。
